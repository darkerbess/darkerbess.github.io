Economy

As one of the world's largest economies, it's a major player in Latin America. Key industries include agriculture (coffee, soybeans, sugarcane), mining (iron ore, bauxite), and manufacturing (automobiles, electronics). Brazil is also a top exporter of beef and poultry. Despite its potential, the economy faces challenges like income inequality, high public debt, and occasional fluctuations due to global commodity prices. Nevertheless, Brazil remains a significant economic force with immense growth opportunities.
